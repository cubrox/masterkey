Master Key

A reading experience tuned for neurodivergent readers — and anyone else who struggles to focus on long-form text.

Paste or upload any passage from the Bahá'í writings or messages from Bahá'í institutions. Master Key renders the text in a comfortable, configurable reading surface with your choice of font, size, contrast, line spacing, and column width — and offers a quick comprehension check at the end so you know what you absorbed.
